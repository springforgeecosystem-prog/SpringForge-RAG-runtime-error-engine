## Metric Definitions

- **Precision@1**: Fraction of top results that are actually relevant to the error query
- **Mean Reciprocal Rank (MRR)**: How quickly the system finds the first relevant document (higher = faster)
- **Recall@5**: Fraction of all relevant documents captured in top 5 results
- **ROUGE-1**: Word-level similarity between generated and reference solutions
